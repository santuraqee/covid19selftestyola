About GDG Yola

Google Developer Group, (GDG) Yola is a group of like minded developers using Google's products to solve problems in their communities and help create an outreach platform for their developers. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

By joining GDG Yola, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
